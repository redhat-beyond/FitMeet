Introducing FitMeet, the ultimate sports app for hanging out with friends! Say goodbye to the hassle of organizing sports events through WhatsApp groups. With FitMeet, you can easily schedule your sports activities and track your friends' availability in real-time. Simply suggest a time that works for you and let your friends suggest new time slots if needed. FitMeet makes it easy to pick the best time and know who's coming, so you can focus on enjoying the game with your friends
